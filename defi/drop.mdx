## Liquid Staking Powered by Neutron

<div className="p-4 border-l-4 border-primary bg-primary/5 rounded my-6">

## Neutron Integration

Drop Protocol showcases the power of Neutron's integrated architecture by utilizing several core modules:

<div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 mt-8">
  <div className="flex flex-col p-6 border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg">
    <h3 className="text-xl font-semibold mb-2">Interchain Transactions (ICTX)</h3>
    <p className="mb-4">Enables Drop to control staking operations across multiple chains without requiring separate validators or infrastructure for each chain.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="flex flex-col p-6 border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg">
    <h3 className="text-xl font-semibold mb-2">Interchain Queries (ICQ)</h3>
    <p className="mb-4">Allows Drop to fetch and verify staking data from source chains in a trust-minimized way, ensuring accurate reward calculations and security.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="flex flex-col p-6 border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg">
    <h3 className="text-xl font-semibold mb-2">Duality DEX</h3>
    <p className="mb-4">Provides deep liquidity pools for dAssets (Drop's liquid staking tokens), enabling efficient trading with minimal slippage.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="flex flex-col p-6 border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-800 rounded-lg">
    <h3 className="text-xl font-semibold mb-2">Supervaults Integration</h3>
    <p className="mb-4">Allows dAssets to be deployed in Supervaults for additional yield on top of staking rewards, enhancing capital efficiency.</p>
  </div>
</div>
